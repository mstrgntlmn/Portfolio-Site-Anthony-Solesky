My Story

I have always had a love for creating. Art, games, music, anything that brings joy to my audience. Nothing gets me more excited than seeing peoples’ reactions to my work. Now, after over a decade of working in IT, it’s about time I turned that hobby into a career. UX Design has allowed me to marry my creative and analytical sides and bring the full force of my talents to bear.

I spent my childhood in Baltimore, until my family moved to Raleigh in ‘96. Then once I got out on my own, I made my way across the country, from Nashville to Kansas City, before settling in Phoenix, AZ. I like to think I carry a little bit of each of those cities with me and allow their unique cultures to influence my work and who I am.
